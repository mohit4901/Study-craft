Meet the Visionary Behind StudyCraft

Mohit Mudgil

Founder & CEO, StudyCraft

Mohit is a passionate educator and technology enthusiast with a vision to revolutionize the way students learn and prepare for exams. With a background in Computer Science, Education, Web development, he identified the challenges students face in managing vast syllabi and maintaining motivation.

Driven by the belief that personalized learning can unlock every student's potential, Mohit embarked on the journey to create StudyCraft. The platform is a culmination of years of research, dedication, and a deep understanding of effective study methodologies, powered by cutting-edge AI.

Their commitment extends beyond just building an app; it's about fostering a community of empowered learners. Mohit continues to lead StudyCraft with innovation at its core, constantly seeking new ways to make academic success accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the leading AI-powered platform that transforms traditional study methods into dynamic, personalized, and highly effective learning journeys for students worldwide.

Mission: To empower every student with intelligent tools that simplify syllabus management, create adaptive study plans, provide instant AI tutoring, and offer clear progress tracking, fostering confident and successful learners.
